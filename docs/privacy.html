Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Mosser Watch is a notification application developed by Elizabeth Breeden.

Information Collected

Mosser Watch may collect a mobile phone number from users who choose to receive SMS alerts. The application also stores identifiers for previously seen eBay listings to prevent duplicate notifications.

How Information Is Used

Mobile phone numbers are used only to send Mosser Watch SMS notifications about matching eBay listings.

Mobile Number Privacy

Mobile numbers collected through Mosser Watch are not sold, rented, shared, or disclosed to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on newly detected matching eBay listings.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply.

Opt Out

Users may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message.

Help

Users may reply HELP for assistance.
